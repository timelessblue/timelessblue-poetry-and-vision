                                    51. Lan Yue(Shirley) majored in Fashion Design, then business management, and has been engaged in European international fashion brands business for more than 30 years. By chance, she entered the art of porcelain painting, and became the founder of %u201cTimeless Blue Artwork Co.%u201d and the convener of %u201cContemporary Originality Porcelain Artist Union%u201d.2. The works of her art are infinitely creative, outstanding in craftsmanship, elegant, and timeless, containing the charm of oriental classical poetry culture and Western romantic elements. The forms are diverse and never repeated, which is evocative and thought-provoking. The original construction method %u201ccloud dyeing%u201d and the soil pile technology show a unique three-dimensional and natural gradient effect on porcelain, bringing an amazing breakthrough to porcelain painting.3. She loves poetry and literature, and She has been committing to promoting humanities and art. She operated the Facebook fan pages %u201cSpontaneous Poetry %u201c and the artistic fan page %u201cTimeless Blue%u201d. At the same time, she is also a producer of popular art self-media videos. With the support of well-known kiln factories and art centers, she produced the art program %u201cArt Window%u201d, and cooperated with wellknown artists and professional talents in the industry to promote contemporary aesthetics through video interviews.4. Lan Yue(Shirley) senses that there was a lack of public information on porcelain art. To promote the art of color painting porcelain, she held many public lectures in the Rotary Clubs and other major clubs with practical actions, actively promoting the art of porcelain painting art.5. Her artworks are welcomed by collectors, at the beginning of 2021, it%u2019s been regularly exhibited at %u201cVeryartist Gallery%u201d, and the largest porcelain gallery chain Tai-Hwa Pottery. The brand %u201cTimeless Blue%u201d was established then, and also set up the official website.Spontaneous Poetry Facebook fan pagesTimeless BlueOffical web6. On April 7, 2022, she officially became the first porcelain artist in Taiwan to issue NFT porcelain and sold out in 6 hours.7. On December 20, 2022, she became the first porcelain artist in Taiwan to publish a complete online course on porcelain art concepts and creations.9. In 2023, she was invited to participate in the %u201cOde to the Master%u201d exhibition at the Fo Guang Yuan Museum of Art in Taipei and exhibited her works in memory of Master Hsing Yun. She paints a large porcelain plate in memory of Master Hsing Yun%u2019s %u201cThe Great Wish of Compassion%u201d weighing more than 10 kilograms. It is currently collected in the Fo Guang Yuan Museum of Art in Taipei, and has been reported by Human TV and Human World Fubao interview report.10. She received The %u201cCultural and Creative Elite Award%u201d from the Taipei Painting and Calligraphy Artists Professional Union in September 2023.11. She%u2019s the member of Asia Art Alliance, and will be participating the preparation of 15th Asia Alliance Exhibition 2025 Taoyuan, and hosting the welcome events.8. It passed the strict evaluation of the headquarter of Fo Guang Yuan Museum (which owns 28 museums in the world) in September 2022 and will be exhibited at the FGY Taipei Museum in 2025.
